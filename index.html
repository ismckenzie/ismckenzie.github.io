About

I work at the intersection of discourse analysis, philosophy of language, and literary theory. The Prometheus Project is an ongoing series of essays exploring the relationship between humanity and language — drawing on French theory (Benveniste, Ricœur, Barthes) while remaining accessible to anyone curious about how language shapes thought, identity, and culture.
